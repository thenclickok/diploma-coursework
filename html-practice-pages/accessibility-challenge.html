Explore the Natural Beauty of London

Take a walk through one of London's vibrant green spaces. Breathe in the fresh air while listening to the birdsong of the resident blackbirds as you stroll through St James Park. Take a walk through Hyde Park and you may spot swans, squirrels and even the odd red fox from time to time. Take a trip through Regent's Park in the springtime if you wish to see the delicate cherry blossoms in full bloom.
